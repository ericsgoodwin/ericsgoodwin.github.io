About

Eric is a geospatial data analyst living in Salt Lake City, UT.
Originally from Tennessee, he started his geospatial career with the Tennessee Department of Transportation in 2020 as a GIS Technician before pursuing a Master of Science in Geography at the University of Utah, where he researched the ability to detect power outages from nighttime lights imagery. Since 2023, Eric has worked in the DIGIT Lab as a GIS Analyst where he fulfills mapping, analysis, and research requests for clients from a broad array of industries.
